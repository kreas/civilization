## Step 1: Discovery

- **Title:** 1. Discovery — Understanding before building
- **Body:** Every engagement starts here. Before a single line of code is written, our strategists spend 4–6 weeks embedded in your world. We audit your systems, map your workflows, talk to your team, and define exactly what success looks like. Discovery isn't a formality — it's the phase that determines whether everything after it succeeds or fails. By the end, you have a technical roadmap, a clear business case, and full confidence that the plan is right.

### Detail Cards (2-column grid)

**Card 1: Technical Audit**
We review your current systems, architecture, data, and infrastructure. We identify what's working, what's fragile, what's undocumented, and what's blocking you from moving forward.

**Card 2: Workflow & Risk Mapping**
We document how your business actually operates — not how the org chart says it should. We surface integration risks, data migration challenges, and capacity constraints before they become surprises.

---

## Step 2: Implementation — The Dark Factory

- **Title:** 2. Implementation — AI agents build. Humans oversee.
- **Body:** Once Discovery is complete and the roadmap is locked, we flip the switch. Our AI agents take over implementation — writing code, running tests, integrating with your systems, and iterating on builds around the clock. Our human strategists stay involved for architecture decisions, quality assurance, and client communication. This is the Dark Factory in action: deterministic, consistent, and fast.

### Detail Grid (3-column)

**Column 1:**
- **Label:** BUILD CYCLE
- **Title:** Continuous iteration
- **Description:** AI agents ship working builds daily, not on a two-week sprint cycle. Progress is visible and measurable from day one.

**Column 2:**
- **Label:** OVERSIGHT
- **Title:** Human-in-the-loop
- **Description:** Every architectural decision and quality gate is reviewed by our strategists. AI handles volume. Humans handle judgment.

**Column 3:**
- **Label:** QUALITY
- **Title:** Production-grade from the start
- **Description:** Automated testing, documentation, and code review are built into every build cycle — not bolted on at the end.

---

## Step 3: Delivery & Handoff

- **Title:** 3. Delivery — Your software, your team, your future
- **Body:** A project doesn't end when the code is deployed. We enter a structured handoff phase to make sure your team can run, maintain, and evolve the software we built. You get full documentation, training, and a transition plan. We don't build black boxes — we build systems your people can own.

### Detail Cards (2-column grid)

**Card 1: Documentation & Training**
Complete technical documentation, architecture diagrams, and hands-on training for your team. Everything they need to maintain and extend the system without us.

**Card 2: Support Transition**
A defined support window after launch where we're available for bug fixes, performance tuning, and knowledge transfer. When we leave, you're not stranded.
