## Section 1: Discovery & Strategy

*Positioned as: the entry point to every engagement AND a standalone paid service*

- **Section Headline:** Discovery first. Always.
- **Body:** We don't start with code. We start with questions. What's broken? What's manual? Where does AI actually create value — and where is it just hype? Discovery is a 4–6 week engagement where our strategists audit your systems, map your workflows, identify risks, and define what success looks like. You walk away with a technical roadmap, a clear business case, and confidence that the plan is right before a dollar is spent on implementation.
- **Callout:** Discovery is included in every build engagement. It's also available as a standalone service for teams that need strategic clarity before committing to a full project.

### What You Get

1. **Technical Audit** — A thorough review of your current systems, architecture, and data. We identify what's working, what's fragile, and what's holding you back.
2. **Workflow Mapping** — We document how your business actually operates — not how the org chart says it should. This is where the real opportunities surface.
3. **Risk Assessment** — Integration complexity, data migration challenges, team capacity constraints. We surface the risks upfront so there are no surprises later.
4. **Strategic Roadmap** — A detailed plan with prioritized phases, timelines, cost estimates, and success criteria. This becomes the blueprint for everything that follows.

### Standalone Discovery

- **Duration:** 4–6 weeks
- **Deliverable:** Technical roadmap, business case, risk assessment, and implementation plan
- **Ideal for:** Companies that know they need to modernize or integrate AI but aren't ready to commit to a full build. Discovery gives you the clarity to make that decision with confidence.

## How We Build: The Dark Factory

*Woven-in methodology section — not a service, but the "how" behind all three*

- **Section Headline:** Why we're faster.
- **Body:** Traditional software development is slow because humans do everything — write the code, review the code, test the code, fix the code. We separated the work. Our humans handle Discovery, architecture, and strategic oversight. Our AI agents handle implementation — writing code, running tests, iterating on builds around the clock. We call it the Dark Factory, borrowed from manufacturing where fully automated facilities run with the lights off. The result isn't just speed. It's consistency, quality, and a fundamentally different cost structure.

### By the Numbers

- **50–70% faster** delivery compared to traditional dev shops
- **30–50% lower cost** by replacing developer headcount with AI agent capacity
- **Production-grade quality** enforced by automated testing, human oversight, and Discovery-led requirements
